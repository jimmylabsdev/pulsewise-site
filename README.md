# pulsewise-site

Marketing site and legal pages for PulseWise — a private, voice-first blood pressure tracker.

Live at [pulsewise.vigshrav.com](https://pulsewise.vigshrav.com).

## Structure

- `index.html` — landing page
- `privacy.html` — privacy policy (linked from Play Store listing)
- `disclaimer.html` — medical disclaimer
- `styles.css` — shared styles
- `images/` — icon and screenshots

## Deploy

Auto-deploys to Cloudflare Pages on every push to `main`.
