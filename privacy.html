Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What we collect

PulseWise collects only the blood pressure readings you enter — systolic, diastolic, pulse, date, time, and any notes you add. We do not collect your name, email, location, device identifiers, or any other personal information.

Where your data is stored

All your readings are stored locally on your device. Your data never leaves your device for storage purposes. There is no cloud sync, no user account, and no remote backup.

Voice transcripts

When you use voice entry, the audio is transcribed on-device by your phone's speech recognition. If the on-device parser cannot extract the numbers, we use AI to extract the BP values from the transcript text. Neither the transcript nor the extracted values are stored on our servers or with the AI provider — they are processed in memory and discarded immediately after the values are returned.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share your data with advertisers.
  • We do not track your usage or behavior.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs.
  • We do not require a user account.

Exporting your data

You can export all your readings as a PDF report or CSV file at any time from Settings → Export. The exported file is yours to keep, share with your doctor, or back up manually.

Deleting your data

Individual readings can be deleted from the History tab. To delete all your data at once, uninstall the app — this removes the local data permanently. Since we store nothing on our servers, uninstalling is equivalent to a full account deletion.

Account deletion requests

Since PulseWise does not maintain user accounts, there is no account to delete. If you have specific concerns or want to confirm no data is held about you, email pulsewise@vigshrav.com and we will respond within 7 days.

Children

PulseWise is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a future update, the new version will be shown in the app and on this page. Continued use after an update means you accept the revised policy.

Contact

Questions, concerns, or anything you'd like clarified: pulsewise@vigshrav.com
