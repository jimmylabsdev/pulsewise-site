Medical Disclaimer

Please read carefully

Not a medical device

PulseWise is not a medical device or diagnostic tool. It does not measure, diagnose, treat, or prevent any medical condition.

What this app is for

PulseWise is designed solely to help you log and track blood pressure readings taken with your own validated BP monitor. The readings, trends, and summaries shown in the app are for personal record-keeping only.

Not medical advice

Nothing shown in PulseWise should be used to make decisions about your health, medication, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for interpretation of your readings and for any medical concerns.

In an emergency

If you are experiencing a medical emergency, contact your local emergency services immediately. Do not rely on this app.

Reference values

The BP categories shown in the app (Low, Normal, High) are simplified thresholds for quick orientation, not clinical guidelines. Your doctor's interpretation of your readings, in the context of your full medical history, is what matters.

Questions
